This isn't "official" since there's no witnesses or anything like that, but in the event I die and people are left wondering as to what I want to do with the money and body, here it is. When I die, my next of kin (my biological mother, the very same on my birth certificate) is set to receive $500,000. Here's what I want with the money, in the order of it to be used.

- Pay off any and all existing debts I have (student loans, unpaid bills, etc)
- Pay for a funeral. I would like a small service, being as least expensive as possible. On my tombstone, I would like my full name, and a symbol (inscribed on my dogtags).
- Allocate $100,000 for my youngest sister (who also has my biological mother's name on her birth certificate) to pay for college, a car, a laptop, and other expenses related to college.
- Allocate another $100,000 for the college fund of the children (who has the name of my oldest sister on their birth certificate) of my oldest sister (the sister who also has my biological mother's name on her birth certificate).
- The remaining money is to be left to my mother to cover retirement, additional college funds for any future children of my siblings (who have the name of my biological mother on their birth certificate) along with family emergencies (accidents, medical bills, etc) at her discretion.

My body is to be buried in the ground as is. I would like to be buried in my army uniform, and have my dogtags around my neck. My eyes are to be closed and my wounds to be hidden from view if possible. I would like a pack of american spirits yellows to be placed in my left ankle pocket of my uniform along with a lighter.

I would like any immediate family to be present at the funeral. Friends of mine are permitted to attend as well, and are to be sent an invitation. I would like the members of my unit to be sent invitations as well, along with any surviving members of my basic training unit and AIT unit.

As for my possessions, I would like them to be shared amongst my surviving siblings and their future children. They can be sold for money if need be. The electronics of mine (laptop, tablets, phone) are to be destroyed, or in the very least their harddrives/solid state drives to be crushed and incinerated. Any remaining funds in my bank account are to be distributed equally amongst my surviving siblings.

If I am to be left in a state where I'm unresponsive ("a vegetable") I would like to be taken off life support to allow my body to die naturally. If my organs refuse to fail and there's little to no chance of recovery, I would like to be euthanized.

A link to this will has been given to trusted individuals and there are to share it to my family as soon as they learn of my death. If any copies are inconsistent with others, they are to be assumed false, with the majority of the ones that are similar to be accepted as the truth, and my final will.

A copy of this will can be found on my website (https://taizweb.github.io/post/will.txt), this electronic note, a handwritten note in my wallet, and an envelope hidden in my things.
